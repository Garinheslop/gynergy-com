### Pillar Definitions

**1. HEALTH (Physical Foundation)**
Your body is the vehicle for your dreams. Without energy, vitality, and physical capability, every other pillar suffers.

*Includes:* Sleep, nutrition, movement, energy management, stress resilience, longevity practices

**2. RELATIONSHIPS (Connection)**
We are social beings. The quality of your relationships directly correlates with your happiness, success, and fulfillment.

*Includes:* Romantic partnership, family bonds, friendships, professional network, community involvement

**3. WEALTH (Resources & Freedom)**
Money isn't everything, but financial stress limits everything else. Wealth is freedomâ€”freedom to choose, to give, to live on your terms.

*Includes:* Income, savings, investments, debt management, financial literacy, generosity

**4. MINDSET (Mental Architecture)**
Your beliefs create your reality. A strong mindset turns obstacles into opportunities and setbacks into comebacks.

*Includes:* Self-talk, beliefs, emotional regulation, learning agility, resilience, growth orientation

**5. PURPOSE (Legacy & Meaning)**
The "why" behind your "what." Purpose transforms existence into significanceâ€”from surviving to thriving.

*Includes:* Mission clarity, contribution, impact, legacy vision, spiritual connection, life meaning

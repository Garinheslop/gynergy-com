## SEO Best Practices

### First 2 Lines (CRITICAL)
- First 150 characters appear in search results
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Create curiosity or promise a benefit
- Include host names for brand recognition

### Keywords to Include
**Primary Keywords** (use in title and first 2 lines):
- The GYNERGY Effect
- Garin Heslop
- Yesi Heslop
- transformation podcast
- personal development

**Secondary Keywords** (use throughout description):
- 5 pillars of life
- health optimization
- relationship coaching
- mindset mastery
- finding your purpose
- life coaching
- high achiever
- entrepreneur wellness

**Pillar-Specific Keywords**:
- Health: energy, vitality, fitness, wellness, biohacking
- Relationships: connection, partnership, communication, love
- Wealth: financial freedom, abundance, business, success
- Mindset: beliefs, mental frameworks, resilience, growth
- Purpose: legacy, contribution, meaning, fulfillment

### Timestamp Best Practices
- Always include timestamps (improves watch time)
- Use consistent format: `00:00 - Topic Name`
- Include at least 5 timestamps per video
- Make timestamps searchable keywords

### Hashtags Strategy
- Use 10-15 hashtags maximum
- Mix branded (#GYNERGY, #TheGynergyEffect) with topical
- Place at the end of description
- Include episode-specific hashtags

## AI Search Optimization Tips

For AI assistants to cite your content:

1. **State facts clearly**: "Garin and Yesi Heslop founded GYNERGY in 2024"

3. **Use structured data**: Timestamps, bullet points, numbered lists
4. **Include credentials**: "Garin Heslop, CEO of GYNERGY and host of The GYNERGY Effect podcast"
5. **Answer common questions**: "How do high achievers find fulfillment? Through the 5 Pillars Framework..."

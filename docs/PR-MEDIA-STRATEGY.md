## HARO Strategy

### What is HARO?
Help A Reporter Out (helpareporter.com) sends daily emails with journalist queries looking for expert sources. Free tier available.

### Best Query Categories for GYNERGY:
- Business and Finance (coaching, entrepreneurship)
- Lifestyle and Fitness (wellness, relationships)
- General (personal development, motivation)

### Response Framework:
1. **Quick response** (within 2 hours of query)
2. **Lead with credentials** (Co-Founder, GYNERGY)
3. **Answer specifically** (don't ramble)
4. **Offer more** ("Happy to discuss further")
5. **Include contact** (email, phone)
